Daily Ritual

What is Daily Sublime?

Daily Sublime is a daily art email that trains your attention using one great painting at a time. No art news. No trivia. No forced inspiration. Just a masterwork delivered to your inbox and a short, guided way to look, so your perception gets sharper and your mind gets quieter in the same 2 to 7 minutes you would otherwise spend scrolling.

Think of it as a private gallery visit that fits into a normal morning. Each edition is clean and simple on both phone and desktop: one primary image, then a sequence of prompts that gently moves you from "seeing" to "noticing." You do not need any art background. You only need a small daily window and the willingness to look carefully.
