The Starry Night Collection

Featured Artist

Vincent van Gogh
The Starry Night · 1889

Van Gogh painted this masterpiece from his asylum room, capturing not what he saw but what he felt, a swirling cosmos of emotion.

Why It Matters

Van Gogh's visible brushstrokes and bold colors broke from academic tradition, paving the way for Expressionism. Every swirl carries emotion.

✦ Key Observations

I
The cypress tree connects earth to sky like a dark flame.
II
Spiraling brushstrokes create cosmic energy throughout the sky.

✦ Today's Practice

Tonight, step outside and look at the stars. Van Gogh saw movement in them. Try to see it too.
